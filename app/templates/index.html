About EDI 855 Purchase Order Acknowledgment

Understanding the critical role of EDI 855 in electronic commerce

Purchase Order Confirmation

The EDI 855 document serves as a formal acknowledgment from suppliers to buyers, confirming receipt and acceptance of purchase orders. It establishes a clear communication channel for order status.

Trading Partner Communication

Facilitates seamless B2B communication by standardizing acknowledgment processes. Partners can quickly understand order status, changes, and any issues that need attention.

Supply Chain Efficiency

Reduces processing time and errors in supply chain operations. Automated acknowledgments enable faster decision-making and improved inventory management.

Key EDI 855 Components

ISA/GS
Interchange Headers

Control segments that identify trading partners and transmission details

ST
Transaction Set Header

Identifies the transaction as EDI 855 Purchase Order Acknowledgment

BAK
Beginning Segment

Contains acknowledgment type, PO number, and acknowledgment date

PO1
Baseline Item Data

Details for each line item including quantities, prices, and product information

ACK
Line Item Acknowledgment

Acknowledgment codes indicating acceptance, rejection, or changes to line items

SE/GE
Transaction Trailers

End-of-transaction markers with segment counts and control numbers
